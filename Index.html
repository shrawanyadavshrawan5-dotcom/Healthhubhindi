Sardi Jukam Ka 5 Min Me Ilaaj

1. Adrak-Tulsi Chai: 1 cup paani + 5 tulsi + adrak ubalo. Din me 3 baar.
2. Haldi Doodh: Raat ko 1 glass garam doodh + 1/2 chammach haldi.
3. Ajwain Ki Bhap: Garam paani me ajwain daal ke bhap lo. Naak khul jayegi.

Gas & Pet Dard Ka Turant Upchar

Ajwain + Kaala Namak: 1 chammach ajwain + chutti bhar kaala namak + garam paani. Khane ke baad piye. 5 min me aaram.

Baal Jhadna 100% Rokne Ka Nuskha

Nariyal Tel + Curry Patta: Tel garam karo, 10 curry patte daalo. Thanda karke jado me lagao. Hafte me 2 baar. 30 din me result.
